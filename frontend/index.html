      GENESIS INFINITY

      Author the world. The engine plays it.

      A tiny AI-run tabletop RPG. Pick a world, say what your character does in plain English, and the engine adjudicates and narrates what happens.

      1. 1Pick a world. Tap the title up top to choose an Experience — action, fantasy, sci-fi, romance, medieval, or cyberpunk.
      2. 2Wait for the model. The dot by the model name turns green when it's ready.
      3. 3Take a turn. Type what your character does (“sprint to the rooftop and grapple across”) — not a command. The engine decides if it works.
      4. 4Check your sheet. The person icon in the message bar opens your HP, location, and inventory.
